E-Paper Calendar — Device Flasher

Chrome or Edge on desktop. Plug the board in over USB-C.

  1. Flash the firmware

    Pick the device's serial port when prompted, then "Install". When it finishes, close the dialog and continue below.

  2. Send Wi-Fi & server

    Re-select the same serial port. The board must be on its "waiting for CFG" prompt (it is right after flashing, or after a power-cycle while unconfigured).
